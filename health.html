This session runs from 1:00-2:30 and focuses on health data reporting and analysis, with a focus on Covid-19. Tentative speakers are:

  • Casey Briggs TBD
  • Liam Mannix Model Madness: the difficulty of communicating and reporting on modelling

    Modelling played a key role in our pandemic response. But how effectively was it communicated to the public? As a science reporter who did most of The Age and the Sydney Morning Herald's modelling reporting, my suspicion is: very poorly. There are several reasons for this that I think can inform how we go forward both as journalists and as modellers.
